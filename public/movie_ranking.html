My top 3 movies of all-time.

Her

"Her" is a 2013 sci-fi romance about a lonely writer who falls in love with his advanced AI operating system..

Ex Machina

"Ex Machina" is a sci-fi thriller about a programmer who interacts with a highly advanced and potentially dangerous humanoid AI.

Transcendence

"Transcendence" is a sci-fi thriller where a scientist's consciousness is uploaded into a superintelligent AI, leading to unforeseen consequences.
